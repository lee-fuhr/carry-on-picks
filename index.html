How we choose our picks

We base our recommendations on a combination of factors: the brand’s reputation among frequent travelers and airline crew, long-term owner reviews that reveal which parts fail first (typically wheels and telescoping handles), and the assessments of professional travel gear reviewers. We do not rely on short-term lab tests or single-use impressions. Instead, we look for patterns in feedback across thousands of users over months and years of use. A bag that looks great in the showroom but has a zipper that sticks after a dozen trips doesn’t make our list. We prioritize durability, repairability, and real-world ergonomics.
